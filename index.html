Hi. I am Ayetri Roy, an aspiring contemporary artist based in New York. A feminist at heart, I promote social issues through my paintings, which are mainly themed by strong, independent women. Occasionally, I also paint about the day-to-day things I encounter in my life. As you will see, I have hopped around the world quite a bit!

I am originally from Rangamati in Bangladesh, the daughter of the Chakma Chief, Raja Devasish Roy, and the late Rani Tatu Roy. In my childhood, I moved to the capital, Dhaka, to study at Green Herald School. I then moved around between Dhaka, Bangkok and Kodaikanal (India) to finally complete my high school studies in IB. Currently, I am pursuing my BBA at Monroe College in New Rochelle, New York.
